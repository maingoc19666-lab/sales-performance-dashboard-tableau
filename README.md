# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard for analyzing business performance. The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, customer segments, products, sales channels, and regional performance to support business decision-making.

## Objectives

- Analyze overall revenue and profit
- Monitor business performance over time
- Compare sales performance across regions
- Evaluate sales channels and payment methods
- Identify top-performing products
- Understand customer segmentation
- Support strategic business decisions through data visualization

## Tools

- Tableau Desktop
- Tableau Hyper Extract
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Overall Business Performance
- Revenue
- Profit
- Profit Margin
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 2. Time Analysis
- Revenue trends
- Profit trends
- Business performance over time

### 3. Regional Analysis
- Revenue by region
- Geographic sales distribution
- Warehouse performance

### 4. Product Analysis
- Revenue by product
- Profit by product category
- Product quantity sold
- Top-performing products

### 5. Customer Analysis
- Revenue by customer segment
- Customer distribution by region
- Customer channels

### 6. Sales Channel Analysis
- Sales channel comparison
- Payment methods
- Revenue contribution by channel

## Key Insights

The dashboard enables users to:

- Track business growth over time.
- Compare revenue and profitability across regions.
- Identify the best-performing products.
- Analyze customer segments and purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate the effectiveness of different sales channels.
